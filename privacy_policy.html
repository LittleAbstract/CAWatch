CAWatch Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 15, 2026
App: CAWatch (package com.security.careactivator)

CAWatch is a security tool that watches your device's trusted certificate store and notifies you when a new user-installed Certificate Authority (CA) appears. This policy explains what the app does and does not do with your data.

1. Data we collect

CAWatch stores only on your device a local baseline of the Certificate Authorities already trusted on your phone. For each CA this includes its issuer name, subject name, SHA-256 fingerprint, and the time it was first detected. This is device/security state metadata — it is not your identity, contacts, location, or any other personal information.

2. Data we share or transmit

None. CAWatch makes no network connections. It has no internet permission, no analytics, and no advertising SDKs. The CA metadata described above never leaves your device. You can clear it at any time with the in-app "Reset baseline" action, or wipe everything by uninstalling the app.

3. Permissions we use

  • Foreground service (special use) — only for the optional "Always-on" monitoring mode, which runs a lightweight foreground service to continuously watch the trust store and show a persistent status notification.
  • Receive boot completed — so monitoring can resume after a device restart and the baseline comparison stays current.
  • Post notifications — to alert you when a new user-installed CA is detected.

No location, camera, microphone, contacts, storage, or other sensitive permissions are requested.

4. Subscriptions

CAWatch may offer an optional subscription (e.g. a free trial followed by a monthly plan) to unlock the "Always-on" monitoring mode. All payments are processed by Google Play through the Google Play Billing system. CAWatch itself does not collect, store, or transmit any payment information. Subscription management, including cancellation and refunds, is handled entirely by Google Play.

5. Children

CAWatch is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect any data from children.
